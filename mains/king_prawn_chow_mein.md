# King Prawn Chow Mein

Recipe by Roinnsenn

## Ingredients

### Marinate the prawns
- 16 Prawns
- 1/4 tsp White Pepper
- 1/2 tsp Rice Wine
- 1/4 tsp Salt
- 1 tsp Corn Starch

### Stir Fry noodle
- 200g Egg Noodles
- 150g Bean Sprouts
- 3 Spring Onions
- 1 Onion
- 1 tbsp Oil
- 3 Cloves of Garlic
- 1/2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1/2 tbsp Rice Wine
- Black Pepper

## Method

1. Marinate the prawns with white pepper, rice wine, salt and corn starch for 10 minutes.
2. Press the garlic, finely chop the spring onions and slice the onion.
3. Heat up the oil with medium-low heat, fry the spring onions (white) and garlic.
4. Cook the prawns, and set them aside.
5. Fry the onions until fully cooked.
6. Add egg noodles and stir fry for 2 minutes with some oil.
7. Add oyster sauce, rice wine and soy sauce, stir fry for 2 minutes. 
8. Put the prawns back and fry for 20 seconds.
9. Add some spring onions and black pepper, stir fry for another 2 minutes.
